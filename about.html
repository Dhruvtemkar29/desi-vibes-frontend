Our Story

The journey from concern to creation

The Beginning

In 2019, our lives took a beautiful turn with the arrival of our baby boy. As new parents, we wanted only the best for him, so we purchased what we thought were the best baby soaps available. However, we soon learned that commercial products contain chemicals that aren't ideal for delicate skin.

The Discovery

We began exploring organic alternatives and discovered homemade soaps prepared with love and care by local homemakers. The difference was remarkable – our baby's skin responded beautifully to these natural products.

The Creation

Inspired by this discovery, we decided to create our own handmade soaps. After extensive research and training, we crafted our first batch with natural ingredients. The results were astounding – soft, nourished skin without any harsh chemicals.

The Growth

Today, Desi Vibes proudly manufactures 18 categories of homemade soaps, each carefully crafted with love and natural ingredients. Our mission continues to expand as we work on introducing more home essentials, including our next venture – aromatic wax candles.
